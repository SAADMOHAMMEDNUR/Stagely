Stagely Project Proposal

Problem Description / Data Domain

The Super Bowl is one of the biggest events in the United States, but many people do not actually watch it for the football. Instead, they are more interested in the halftime show, commercials, or just having something fun to do while watching with friends. For these viewers, the game itself can feel boring or hard to follow.

This creates an information and engagement problem. While the Super Bowl includes repeated phrases, famous performers, and memorable ads, there is no organized way for users to interact with these moments. Most discussion happens on social media, where opinions are scattered and quickly lost. Existing websites may post polls or articles, but they are usually passive and do not let users meaningfully interact with the content.

A data-driven web app that organizes Super Bowl-related information into interactive features could make the event more engaging, especially for people who are not football fans.

App Description

Stagely is an interactive website designed to make Super Bowl Sunday more fun for casual viewers, music fans, and people who may not care much about football. The app gives users something engaging to do during the game instead of just passively watching.

One main feature of Stagely is a Super Bowl Buzzword Bingo game. Users receive a randomly generated bingo card filled with common Super Bowl words and phrases. As these moments happen during the game, users can click the matching squares on their card. The app tracks progress and checks when users complete rows or get bingo.

Another major feature is a voting system for halftime performers and Super Bowl commercials. Artists and ads are displayed as cards, and users can vote for their favorites. Votes are stored and used to create live rankings that users can sort or filter.

As a smaller feature, users can customize their bingo card by selecting categories they care about most, such as music, ads, or football-related terms. This allows users to personalize their experience and supports the main bingo feature.

App Features

  • Buzzword Bingo Game: Users interact with a bingo card by clicking buzzwords as they happen during the Super Bowl. The app tracks progress and checks for completed rows or bingo.
  • Voting and Rankings: Users vote for halftime performers and commercials. Votes are aggregated and displayed in live rankings that can be sorted and filtered.
  • Custom Bingo Cards (Small Feature): Users choose categories to generate a personalized bingo card based on their interests.

Information From an External Source

The app will use a static CSV file to store Super Bowl buzzwords, halftime performers, and advertisements. User interactions such as votes and bingo progress will be tracked and used to update the interface dynamically.
